# Tournament Name Ideas

A shortlist of candidate names for the yearly student golf and networking event in Stavanger. Names are grouped by style so a brand direction can be chosen before narrowing down.

## Classic & Descriptive

Straightforward names that clearly communicate what the event is. Easy to explain to sponsors and students.

- Stavanger Student Golf Invitational
- Stavanger Student Open
- Stavanger Student Golf Classic
- Stavanger Student Championship
- Stavanger Business & Student Open
- The Stavanger Student Cup
- Stavanger Student Scramble
- Stavanger Student Golf Trophy

## Regional & Local Character

Names that lean into Stavanger's identity, coastline, and regional pride.

- Rogaland Student Open
- Nordsjø Student Open ("North Sea Student Open")
- Jærlinken Student Cup (nodding to the Jæren landscape)
- Preikestolen Invitational
- Fjord & Fairway Invitational
- The Stavanger Links Invitational
- Sola Student Open (if hosted at Sola Golfklubb)
- Vestland Student Cup

## Networking & Career Angle

- The Talent Invitational
- Fairway Connect Stavanger
- The Career Classic
- Next Generation Open

- Bridge Open Stavanger

## Naming Considerations

Points to weigh before committing to a name:

- Works in both Norwegian and English marketing.
- Domain and social handle availability.
- Room to grow beyond Stavanger if the event expands regionally.
- Sponsor-friendly (a headline sponsor may want naming rights, e.g. "SpareBank 1 SR-Bank Student Open").
- Short enough to fit on scorecards, name badges, and merchandise.
- Not easily confused with existing Norwegian golf events or student competitions.

## Recommended Shortlist

If a quick decision is needed, these three balance clarity, brandability, and local identity:

1. **Stavanger Student Invitational** — clear, professional, sponsor-friendly.
2. **Nordsjø Student Open** — distinctive regional identity, memorable.
3. **The Connection Cup** — leans into the networking mission and scales beyond golf.
