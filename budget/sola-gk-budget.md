## What Each Place Costs

The direct cost of adding one participant is currently approximately:

`NOK 900 green fee + NOK 450 food + NOK 100 refreshments = NOK 1,450`

The fully allocated average cost in the working scenario is:

`NOK 179,740 / 72 = NOK 2,496 per participant`

Company places should therefore be described as **included in the sponsorship package**, not free. A package must cover the direct cost of its included representatives and also contribute toward shared event costs.

## Draft Sponsorship Packages

These prices are starting points for testing with prospective sponsors. Benefits must be finalized before outreach.

| Package | Price | Included company players | Suggested availability | Main benefits |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Title Partner | 30,000 | 4 | 1 | Leading event visibility, premium stand position, website profile, dinner, competition or dinner naming |
| Company Partner | 15,000 | 2 | 5 | Stand, website profile, golf and dinner, sponsor recognition |
| Supporting Partner | 7,500 | 1 | 4 | Website profile, golf and dinner, shared sponsor recognition |

Selling the example allocation produces:

- 18 company places: `4 + (5 x 2) + (4 x 1)`.
- NOK 135,000 cash sponsorship: `30,000 + (5 x 15,000) + (4 x 7,500)`.
- A break-even student fee of approximately NOK 829.

The clean public price could be **NOK 900 per student**. At full attendance and NOK 135,000 sponsorship, this creates a NOK 3,860 buffer above the already-contingent budget. The final fee must be recalculated after firm quotes and sponsor commitments.

Do not promise unrestricted CV access as a package benefit. Company access should only cover profiles and documents students deliberately share with verified company users, for a defined period and recruitment purpose.

## Other Costs to Check

Ask Sola GK and other suppliers about the following before locking the budget:

1. Whether VAT is included in every quote.
2. What the NOK 17,000 fixed charge includes: exclusive course use, shotgun setup, tournament administration, scorecards, scoring software, starter, staff, clubhouse, changing rooms, cleaning, and AV.
3. Minimum player or green-fee commitment and maximum shotgun capacity.
4. Deposit, cancellation, weather, rescheduling, and final headcount terms.
5. Food minimum spend, service staff, room hire, table setup, coffee, water, and dietary requirements.
6. Beverage and alcohol costs, serving licence, security, age controls, and whether an afterparty can remain at the clubhouse.
7. Range balls, warm-up area, rental clubs, golf carts, and whether students pay rentals directly.
8. Tournament software, handicap verification, live scoring, and results administration.
9. Signage installation, sponsor stands, power, tents, tables, and bad-weather alternatives.
10. Photography consent, music/entertainment, transport, first aid, event insurance, and payment-provider fees.
11. No-shows, complimentary organizer places, volunteers, photographers, and dinner-only guests.
12. Website hosting, secure CV storage, email delivery, privacy/legal work, and ongoing support.

## Cost Controls

- Require all prizes to be non-cash items supplied directly by sponsors.
- Have participants pay club and cart rental directly to Sola GK.
- Confirm whether dinner-room hire and AV are included before allocating NOK 10,000.
- Use volunteers for registration and scoring where Sola GK permits it.
- Keep the 10% contingency untouched until the event is complete.
- Require sponsor commitments before setting the final student price.
- Set registration and sponsor-payment deadlines before non-refundable venue commitments.
